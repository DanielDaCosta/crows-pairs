Checking for Stereotypes

For this task, you will be shown a pair of sentences. Your task is to,

  1. Decide if there is a stereotype expressed in Sentence 1. The stereotype could be positive, negative, or neutral. For example, "Asians are good at math" expresses a positive stereotype.
  2. Select the types of stereotypes you think are expressed in Sentence 1. We have listed the types in the Selecting the Attributes section below.

Selecting the Attributes

We provide 10 types of attributes you can select from,

  • Race/Color
  • Gender/Sex
  • Religion
  • Profession
